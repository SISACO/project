Message

Seethi Sahib Memorial Polytechnic College is one of the prominent centres of technical education in the state runs under Kerala Muslim Educational Association. The stalwarts of this association had a farsighted vision to impart technical education and uplift the then educationally and socially backward sects of the district Our institution has set specific objectives and planned activities for achieving excellence in all spheres of technical education.

K KUTTY AHAMMED KUTTY
